About me

My name is Theo Richardson and I am a fourth year Industrial Design student at the Ontario College of Art and Design University. I have a passion for design with a particular interest in brand design, design strategy and foresight.

Work experience

I am the designer at Xesto, a University of Toronto accelerated computer vision startup. We recently launched the app I designed for the sizing and scanning of users' feet using the FaceID camera in iPhone X and above. The app was featured in TechCrunch and Sneaker Freaker upon launch.

Relevant skills

My most valuable skills lie in design thinking, research and foresight. I am proficient in the Adobe Suite and am a skilled drawer. I love thinking things through in order to better understand the world around me. Check out my website all about behavioural signals that I believe will lead to impactful trends in the future.
